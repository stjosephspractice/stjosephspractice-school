About St. Joseph's

Obi Dan Obi, the excellency

Our Story

A great achievement in academic excellency. St. Joseph's Practice Primary School has been nurturing young minds since 2004, providing quality education rooted in Christian values.

📌 Our Mission

To improve and build a safer learning environment in order to address the need of individual child to attain full potentials in education and develop awareness of moral, social and religious values.

👁️ Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered school to equip pupils to be Christ honouring, critical thinkers through the pursuit of academic excellence.
